About 658Meta

658Meta is a Tech start-up that specializes in ERC-721 and ERC-20 compliant non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and fan tokens.
We aim to bring technological and digital innovation to the forefront of the local economic conversation, by capitalizing on what is seen by most experts as the re-advent of the Internet.
658Meta, through the TheMetaCrocs will establish and fund scholarships geared towards encouraging more local interest and involvement in the Tech space, fostering the development of a more globally recognized industry that can compete with the very best players in the space.
Local development is at the core of 658Meta's Vision for the future which is why we strive to engage and employ the very talented and brilliant minds of this and other generations to come, providing tech-centric jobs and job opportunities for our Nation's Youth.

Products, Services and Solutions

Our NFTs are based on 1,962 hand drawn and digitized Crocodiles each having different character traits and designs. But what we are offering will be much more than JPEGs, holders will essentially be "minting" into a community/club with tiered perks attached.
By partnering with a variety of goods and service providers, we will offer our holders discounts, promotions, vouchers and more rewards.
Of the 1,962 MetaCrocs One Thousand (1,000) are Green, Nine Hundred (900) are Black and Sixty-Two (62) are Gold MetaCrocs the rarest and most valuable. Each MetaCroc has been assigned a number from #1494 to #3456. Gold MetaCrocs however, are assigned numbers that correspond with relevant pieces of Jamaican History. For example, MetaCroc #1988 will depict a Hurricane Gilbert themed Gold MetaCroc. Or MetaCroc #1825 will depict a Golden MetaCroc that links back to J. Wray and Nephew and the owner of this MetaCroc could potentially benefit from their J. Wray and Nephew purchases through MetaCroc ownership.
TheMetaCrocs was desgined to serve the growing interest and intrigue locally, and will be presented as the perfect entry point into the market for curious tech-savvy professionals.
We will achieve this by combining digital art with intrinsic value.
